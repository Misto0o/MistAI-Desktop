Frequently Asked Questions

Is MistAI free to use?
Yes! MistAI Desktop Assistant is completely free and open-source. The backend API is also free to use.
Does MistAI work offline?
No, MistAI requires an internet connection to communicate with the AI backend. However, the computer vision and automation features work locally.
Is my data private and secure?
MistAI only sends your voice commands and screen text to the AI backend for processing. Screenshots are processed locally and not uploaded. Your conversations are not stored permanently.
Can I use MistAI on Mac or Linux?
Currently, MistAI is designed for Windows only. The code uses Windows-specific libraries and may not work on other platforms without modifications.
How accurate is the wake word detection?
MistAI uses Google's speech recognition with fuzzy matching. It's quite accurate in quiet environments but may have issues with background noise.
What's the difference between Nova, Sage, and Flux?
Nova: Fast and efficient for general tasks (Gemini 2.5 Flash)
Sage: Better at complex reasoning (Command-R7B)
Flux: Balanced performance (Mistral)
Do I need to know Python?
No! The executable version requires zero coding knowledge. Just download and run.
Can I contribute to the project?
Absolutely! MistAI is open-source. Check out the GitHub repo, submit issues, or create pull requests. Contributions are always welcome!

Troubleshooting

Wake word not responding

Check microphone permissions and ensure wake word toggle is enabled in the UI.

OCR not finding text

OCR (Vision Mode) MistAI supports on-screen text recognition. If OCR is unavailable, MistAI will automatically fall back to recovery strategies and continue running normally.

No text-to-speech

Check system volume settings and restart MistAI if the issue persists.

Connection errors

Verify your internet connection. The status indicator shows connectivity.
